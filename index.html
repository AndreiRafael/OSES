About

OSES is an apocalyptic shooter in which you need to manage ammo and positioning of 3 survivors and destroy numerous hordes of zombies. Select one of many maps available, equip each character with different weapons and hats, and get ready to fight some zombies! How long can you survive?

Features

Unique gameplay

Switch control between characters to overcome many challenges in 4 distinct game modes!


Community content

Create and share maps with the community using the built-in map editor!

Character customization

Customize your characters with different hats, weapons and colors!
